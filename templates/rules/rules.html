How to Play

  1. Select a location for your challenge
  2. Recieve a challenge in the form of a photograph
  3. Identify what landmark or distinctive feature the photograph contains
  4. Find the location that photograph was taken from and stand there
  5. When you are confident you have found the right location, click 'Found it!'
  6. If you have successfully found the location, you will be awarded points!
  7. Take on another challenge or compare your score with other players on the leaderboard.
